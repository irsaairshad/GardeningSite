Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions.

🌱 How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) is available for an additional fee. Seeds ship free on orders over $25.

🔄 What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unused items in original packaging. Plants have a 14-day health guarantee.

🌱 Can I get personalized gardening advice?

Yes! Email us with your questions, and our team of gardening experts will respond within 2 business days with tailored advice.

📦 Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship to the US, Canada, and UK. International seed orders may be subject to customs regulations.
